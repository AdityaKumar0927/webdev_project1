My Hobbies

2D Animation

In my freshman year of highschool, my parents bought me an ipad, so I began sketching characters on procreate and they had recently launched the new feature for animation, so I began to make traditional frame-by-frame animated videos. Then I got tired for animating every frame again and again, though it was still easier for me to animate given I had more advanced tools on procreate compared to doing it on paper. Then I came across toon boom harmony and bought their software, which is an industry-grade software (rick & morty was made on it). I learned how to rig characters on Toon Boom Harmony and even full 360 view characters and animate them for various projects- for instance, during highschool I made a video explaining the value of pi and how to calculate the area of a circle by rigging an animated teacher and doing a voice over. Toon Boom harmony provides the tools to make the voice over more human-like by connecting the lip sync to certain mouth positions, and then automates the task for syncing character's mouth movements to my voice over, which is pretty cool.
